# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the OHAS project respectful, constructive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- being respectful and evidence-based
- giving constructive feedback
- distinguishing criticism of ideas from criticism of people
- remaining open to correction and revision

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory conduct
- bad-faith disruption
- personal attacks
- deliberate misrepresentation of evidence or claims

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code and may remove or reject contributions inconsistent with these standards.

## Reporting

Please report concerns through the contact route documented in `governance/maintainers.md`.
