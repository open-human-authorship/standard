# P3 — Human Authored with Declared AI Support Outside Core Composition

## Status
Draft profile for OHAS v0.1.

## Purpose
This profile is intended for works primarily authored by humans where certain AI-assisted support functions are declared and limited by policy.

## Concept
P3 is not a human-only profile. It is a transparency profile for authors and publishers who want to disclose bounded AI assistance while preserving human primary authorship.

## Examples of Declared Support
- Research assistance
- Outline comparison or brainstorming not directly inserted
- Language cleanup suggestions reviewed and rewritten by the author
- Accessibility assistance
- Metadata or indexing support

## Conditions
- The human author remains the primary intellectual author
- AI assistance must be logged or declared
- Direct machine-generated text included in the final certified scope must be either prohibited or explicitly bounded by repository policy
- The repository or issuer must define the threshold for acceptable assistance

## Required Disclosures
- Nature of each AI-assisted activity
- Tool category or system name where appropriate
- Whether any generated text was directly retained, adapted, or rejected
- Scope statement and final authorship claim

## Evidence Expectations
- Declaration of AI-assisted activities
- Version history
- Review notes or workflow notes where applicable

## Typical Use Cases
- Transparent modern publishing
- Educational pilots
- Comparative authorship research
