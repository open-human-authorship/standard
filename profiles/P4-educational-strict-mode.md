# P4 — Educational Strict Mode

## Status
Draft profile for OHAS v0.1.

## Purpose
This profile is intended for educational settings in which an institution requires strong assurance that the certified text was produced by the learner without generative AI assistance.

## Primary Goals
- Protect assessment integrity
- Support transparent authorship in schools, universities, and training contexts
- Provide a repeatable workflow for supervised writing sessions

## Typical Contexts
- In-class essays
- Capstone assignments
- Thesis sections written under controlled conditions
- Admission or scholarship essays
- Professional certification writing exercises

## Allowed
- Basic file saving and backup
- Non-generative spell checking, if explicitly permitted by the institution
- Accessibility tools approved by institutional policy
- Controlled citation import and bibliography tools
- Handwritten notes created by the learner and logged according to local procedure

## Restricted / Institution-Defined
- Grammar tools with rewrite capability
- Speech-to-text tools
- OCR of student notes
- Controlled network access for references
- Import of pre-existing notes or draft fragments

## Prohibited
- Prompt-based generative AI
- AI paraphrasing or rewriting
- AI summarization used in the submitted text
- AI-generated outlines, thesis statements, paragraphs, or conclusions incorporated into the certified scope
- External text insertion that is not logged and authorized

## Supervision Expectations
P4 should normally be paired with:
- supervised sessions or invigilation
- managed devices or restricted workstations
- clear rules on personal devices
- auditable session logs

## Required Disclosures
- institutional policy in force
- scope of the certified submission
- accommodations granted, where relevant
- any exceptions explicitly approved by the institution

## Evidence Expectations
- candidate declaration
- institutional session record
- version history appropriate to the assurance level
- toolchain declaration
- supervisor or invigilator record where applicable

## Typical Assurance Pairings
- D1 for low-stakes pilots
- D2 for standard controlled coursework
- D3 for high-stakes institutional or certification use

## Notes
Institutions using P4 should publish local guidance on accessibility, academic integrity, privacy, and retention of evidence.
