# Contributing to OHAS

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Open Human Authorship Standard (OHAS).

## Scope of Contributions

Contributions may include:

- editorial improvements
- clarification of normative language
- proposal of new or revised policy profiles
- refinement of assurance levels
- schema improvements
- examples and implementation notes
- audit and governance suggestions
- accessibility and accommodation guidance

## Principles

When contributing, please try to preserve:

- clarity
- traceability
- proportionality
- interoperability
- openness to review
- honest treatment of limitations

## Normative vs Informative Changes

Please clearly indicate whether your proposed change affects:

- **normative content**  
  such as the core standard, profiles, or stabilized schemas

or

- **informative content**  
  such as examples, explanations, FAQs, roadmaps, or operational guidance

## How to Contribute

1. Open an issue for significant changes.
2. Describe the problem, rationale, and expected effect.
3. Submit a pull request referencing the issue when possible.
4. Keep changes focused and explain whether they are editorial, structural, or normative.

## Suggested Pull Request Topics

Examples of useful pull requests:

- clarify ambiguous terminology
- improve assurance-level definitions
- refine profile boundaries
- improve manifest or version-record structure
- document implementation risks
- strengthen repository governance language

## Review Expectations

Contributors should expect review on:

- internal consistency
- compatibility with existing profiles and levels
- impact on examples and schemas
- clarity of language
- downstream interoperability implications

## Conduct

By participating in this repository, you agree to follow `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.

## License of Contributions
