# D4 Audit Checklist

## Status
Informative checklist aligned with `OHAS_STANDARD.md` v0.1.

## Baseline
- [ ] All relevant D3 checks have been completed

## Secure Physical Environment
- [ ] Certified writing room or secure environment is identified
- [ ] Access controls are documented
- [ ] Device restrictions are documented
- [ ] Workstation integrity controls are documented
- [ ] Session supervision records are present where required

## Physical Materials
- [ ] Use of handwritten notes or printed materials is controlled
- [ ] Material intake and output are documented where applicable
- [ ] Scanned or archived physical evidence is traceable

## Environment Assurance
- [ ] Network mode is consistent with D4 expectations
- [ ] Room or workstation change controls are documented
- [ ] Operational procedures support strong custody assurance

## Conclusion
- [ ] Evidence supports D4
- [ ] Evidence supports only D3
- [ ] Corrective action required
- [ ] Insufficient evidence
