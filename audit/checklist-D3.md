# D3 Audit Checklist

## Status
Informative checklist aligned with `OHAS_STANDARD.md` v0.1.

## Baseline
- [ ] All relevant D2 checks have been completed

## Independent Review
- [ ] Independent audit or review record is present
- [ ] Auditor identity, role, or authority is documented
- [ ] Review scope is documented
- [ ] Findings are recorded

## Chain of Custody
- [ ] Evidence bundle integrity is documented
- [ ] Records are traceable from creation to review
- [ ] Any custody gaps are explained

## Procedural Assurance
- [ ] Workflow procedures are documented
- [ ] Exceptions were reviewed
- [ ] Any nonconformities were classified and resolved or recorded

## Conclusion
- [ ] Evidence supports D3
- [ ] Evidence supports only D2
- [ ] Corrective action required
- [ ] Insufficient evidence
